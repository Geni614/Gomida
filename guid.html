Game Guide

How to Play

1

Tap the Coin

Tap the coin as many times as you can within the 60-second time limit. Each tap earns you coins!

2

Watch the Timer

Keep an eye on the countdown timer in the top-right corner. You have 60 seconds to collect as many coins as possible.

3

Track Your Score

Your current coin count is displayed at the top of the screen. Try to beat your previous high score!

Extra Features

4

Invite Friends

Invite your friends to join the game and earn bonus rewards:

2,500 coins for inviting 5 friends
5

Leaderboard

Check the leaderboard to see how you rank against other players and compete for the top spot!

Pro Tips

Speed Tip

Use multiple fingers to tap faster and increase your coin collection rate!

Time Management

The timer turns red in the last 10 seconds - make them count!

Daily Practice

Play regularly to improve your tapping speed and climb the leaderboard rankings.
